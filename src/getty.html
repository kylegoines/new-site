Getty Museum

Acessibility Refined

Brief

Getty Museum is renowned for its extensive collection of art and cultural artifacts, attracting a diverse audience of art enthusiasts, scholars, and the general public. Recognizing the importance of inclusivity, the Getty Museum sought to enhance its online presence by re-designing and building an accessible user experience for everyone.

Through rigorous and extensive research and development, our team delivered a living pattern library within a Storybook environment with automated visual regression, accessibility, and unit tests to ensure stability and interface confidence.
