Nike - About Site

High Impact User-facing

Brief

Nike asked our team to rebuild and redesign: Nike - About site. This site is a high traffic portal for press releases and public relations. Due to the nature of the client and the requirements of the project stability and speed were of the utmost importance.

The project was delivered as a complete UI design system managed and developed in storybook. The application is built in Next.js with server-side generation of pages for increased page load speed as well built in typescript for development assurance and stability.
