Brief

At AREA 17 we had the chance to work with The New York Times to build a unique application experience to manage and track past and future events. Wanting the ability to leverage tagging filtering as well as associating speakers, agendas, and sponsors within the events categorization system.

The design system is backed with a storybook for the stability of user interface and content and the administrator experience is supported with the Twill package for Laravel.
